Why Plain?
Share your views and read the views of others. Tolerance is not about agreeing with othes; sometimes your agree and other times you agree to disagree. The best thing about tolerance is, you et to understand why others think the way they do and allow others to get into your mind to also understand how you think. Plain Social is here for that reason. Everyone! Legal Adults! There seems to be a contrast here. Plain is a free speech platform. Due to the no restriction rule on comments, there has to be an age restriction, thus, this platform has an 18+ restriction.
